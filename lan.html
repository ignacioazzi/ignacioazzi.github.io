LAN Airlines is the flag carrier of Chile, and one of the largest airlines in Latin America.

Client

Project type
Proof of Concept
Work done
UX/UI Design
Industry
Airlines

Introduction and objective of the project

The main objective was to polish the UI, solve legibility issues, and highlight most relevant information like flight number, dates, and price. The first step was to analyze the current UX, take notes of pain points, bad practices, and propose solutions. Then we ran a competitor analysis to check how other services like Skyscanner, Iberia, and KLM were approaching the flow.
